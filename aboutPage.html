My Story

I became interested in web development during high school after discovering how websites were built. I started learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through online tutorials and spent my free time creating simple projects for practice.
My interest grew when I helped a local business build a small website that improved their online presence. Seeing the real impact of my work motivated me to continue developing my skills. Over time, I expanded into frontend and backend development and eventually started working professionally as a web developer, focusing on creating clean, user-friendly digital solutions.
